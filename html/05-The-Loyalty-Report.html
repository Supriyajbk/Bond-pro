Bond Behavioral Append

Enhance what you know with what matters most.

Behavioral Append integrates the most detailed attitudinal and behavioral data directly into your customer profiles — improving how, when, and to whom you engage. It sharpens segmentation, supports more personalized offers, and drives measurable lift in performance. Designed for use across members and non-members alike, it’s a fast track to smarter decisions and stronger results.

Synapze XI + Snowflake Access

Direct access to Bond’s loyalty intelligence ecosystem.

Get year-round access to Bond’s proprietary models, benchmarks, and intelligence tools — including Behavioral Append, Loyalty IQ, and Wallet IQ — through our Snowflake-native environment, powered by Synapze XI. Connect your data, run diagnostics, and generate real-time outputs that help you act fast, target smarter, and perform better.

Customer Experience Index (CEI)

A brand score that goes deeper than satisfaction.

CEI brings together emotional, behavioral, and attitudinal data into a single, unified performance score. It helps brands see what’s truly driving loyalty — and what’s not. With benchmarking built in, CEI provides a clear view of where your brand stands, and where to focus next.

Loyalty IQ™

Understand where your customers engage.

Loyalty IQ maps participation across programs, categories, and competitors. It highlights overlapping behavior, shifting preferences, and areas where your share of loyalty may be at risk. With this intelligence, brands can design smarter strategies that protect and grow their customer base. 

Wallet IQ™

Built to follow the money.

Wallet IQ uses anonymized, card-linked data to show where your customers are spending — inside and outside your program. It reveals competitive leakage, missed opportunities, and new ways to target retention, reactivation, or partnership. Because loyalty isn’t just about what people say — it’s about what they do.
